SOILL Hub

Structured support for Mission Soil Living Labs, from start to scale

SOILL provides structured support across the full journey — coordination, training, knowledge sharing, monitoring, and a digital hub — for existing, emerging, and future Soil Living Labs and Lighthouses across Europe.

Dig deeper into SOILL

Discover the Support Structure for Soil Health Living Labs and Lighthouses, your gateway to insights and resources on the EU-funded living labs and lighthouses leading the transition towards healthy soils by 2030.

SOILL Hub

The SOILL Hub is the digital environment for the growing community of Soil Health Living Labs, Lighthouses, researchers, policymakers, land managers, industry, and civil society. It combines the features of a social network and a collaborative workspace.

Helpdesk

Need expert guidance on Soil Health Living Labs or EU Mission Soil funding? Whether you are a Mission Soil Living Lab representative, an applicant or simply interested in soil health topics, the helpdesk is your go-to resource. Our dedicated team is here to assist.

Stakeholder Mapping

Join the SOILL community! Fill in our Stakeholder Mapping Form and help us connect and map key actors committed to improving soil health across Europe. Your input is essential to build a vibrant, engaged network working towards healthier soils.

Atlas of the Mission Soil Living Labs and Lighthouses

The interactive map is a dynamic and evolving tool that showcases the Mission Soil Living Labs and Lighthouses working toward healthy soils across Europe to support the EU Mission “A Soil Deal for Europe”.
